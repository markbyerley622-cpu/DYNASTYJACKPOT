🎮 How to Play

Simply hold Dynasty Jackpot tokens in your wallet. The more tokens you hold, the higher the prize pool tier you qualify for.

Every few minutes, our smart contract triggers an on-chain draw using Chainlink VRF for fairness.

🔍 Verify Draw Fairness

Each draw result is verified via Chainlink VRF (Verifiable Random Function).

You can cross-check the random number proof and transaction ID directly on BNBScan.

💰 Claiming Rewards

Winners are paid automatically to their connected wallet within minutes of the draw completing.

No manual claiming is required — rewards are instant and trustless.
